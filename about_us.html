ABOUT INDIAN RAILWAYS

Indian Railways is a state-owned national railway system of India, managed by the Ministry of Railways. It is one of the world's largest railway networks, carrying over 23 million passengers and transporting around 3 million tons of freight daily. The railway system plays a vital role in connecting different regions of the country and is crucial for the socio-economic development of India.

Our Vision

To provide safe, efficient, and customer-friendly rail services, ensuring the satisfaction of our passengers while contributing to the economic development of the nation.

Our Mission

- To enhance the railway infrastructure and services for better passenger experience.
- To improve freight services to facilitate the transportation of goods.
- To adopt advanced technologies for better operational efficiency.
- To ensure the safety and security of all passengers.
